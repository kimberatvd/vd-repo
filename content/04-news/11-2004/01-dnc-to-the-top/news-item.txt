Title: dnc: to the top

----

Text: As delegates, dignitaries, and reporters fly into Boston to attend the Democratic National Convention this week, they’ll be greeted by an 7 1/2' x 13' message painted on the roofdeck above Visual Dialogue.

Fritz Klaetke of Visual Dialogue collaborated with installation artist Douglas Weathersby to assert their First Amendment rights and send a message to the delegates and the American public. 

We’ve always felt that if you have an opinion, you should shout it from the rooftops (so to speak).
