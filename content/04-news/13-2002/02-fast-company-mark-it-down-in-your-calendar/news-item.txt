Title: fast company: mark it down in your calendar

----

Text: The 2001 Fast Company calendar designed and edited by Visual Dialogue is featured in the April issue of HOW magazine as a winner in their International Design competition. The spiral-bound book features a silver belly band with marketing info for retail sales which slips off to reveal an orange cover embossed with the grid and “FC2001.” Inside, quotes and artwork selected by Visual Dialogue provide thought -provoking ideas drawn from articles published in the magazine. These articles are archived on the Fast Company web site with the url listed on each spread.
