Title: chef lynch: tasty design

----

Text: You may still be full from the holidays, but if you’re still hungry for good food—with a side of good design—check out the latest from Barbara Lynch. Sportello (Italian for “counter”) brings a contemporary dining and take-away experience to the Fort Point neighborhood. Over the past year Visual Dialogue has collaborated with Chef Lynch on overall brand identity and design including logos, packaging, products, uniforms, menus, signage, and websites for No.9 Park, B&G Oysters, The Butcher Shop, Plum, Stir, 9 at Home, Drink, and Barbara Lynch Gruppo. Getting hungry?
