Title: aiga boston: b(one) of the best

----

Text: From more than 500 entries submitted to the 2009 AIGA BoNE (Best of New England) Show, Visual Dialogue received 4 awards for the following projects at the opening last night:

• Sportello restaurant identity
• Barbara Lynch Gruppo website campaign
• FP3/Drink invitation
• Topic 101 website

Congratulations to our clients and collaborators who helped make it happen.

The exhibition is free and open to the public and is on view until July 8, 2009 at the Bakalar Gallery at Massachusetts College of Art and Design.
