Title: steppin’ it up: best of web 2007

----

Text: Two sites created by Visual Dialogue were just named “Best of Web 2007” by Step magazine. The self-promotional site visualdialogue.com and the site for the leading-edge market research firm Topic 101 were named “must see sites” alongside sites for Toyota, The Museum of Modern Art, The Red Cross, Herman Miller, Art Center, and Adobe. Of the 78 projects selected, Visual Dialogue was one of only 12 firms whose work was recognized more than once.
