Title: from the ground up

----

Text: Visual Dialogue covered a lot of ground in creating the brand identity and website for a new landscape architecture firm specializing in green roofs. The firm, appropriately named Ground, is headed by Shauna Gillies-Smith, previously design director at Martha Schwartz, Inc. and currently teaching at Harvard Graduate School of Design. With their new identity taking root, Ground can now focus on their clients’ projects from Boston to Dubai.
