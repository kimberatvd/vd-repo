Title: making the case... for good design

Text: Projects by Visual Dialogue have picked up several awards—2 gold, 1 silver and an honorable mention—from the Council for Advancement and Support of Education (CASE). The CASE awards are given anually to recognize excellence in communications materials in the field of education. The winning publications were done for the following institutions:

Harvard Graduate School of Education—the 2002 issues of the alumni magazine Ed. received a gold award and the annual report was awarded a silver.

Cardigan Mountain School—the admissions package received a gold award and the Summer Program poster received an honorable mention
