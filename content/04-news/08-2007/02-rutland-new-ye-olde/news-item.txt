Title: 24 rutland: new ye olde

----

Text: After almost two years in the making, our new space is ready for its close up. Visual Dialogue collaborated with architects Stern McCafferty to transform a well-worn 1850 townhouse into a modern studio environment. The interior layout maximizes the space through minimal design—a physical demonstration of our creative philosophy. Stop by the next time you’re in the South End.
