Visual Dialogue has partnered with Climate XChange, a group working to help solve the climate crisis by taxing polluters—oil, natural gas, and coal companies—and returning the revenue generated to the taxpayers and businesses of Massachusetts. We provided communication tools—a new name, responsive website, and video explaining the initiative—to spread the message that Massachusetts can lead the nation by passing a carbon tax to combat climate change.
