Title: jazz appreciation in a box

Text: Jazz: The Smithsonian Anthology, “the single best introduction to America's first great musical form” according to Time magazine (and designed by Visual Dialogue), was just released. Six CDs with 111 tracks trace the musical history while a 200-page book of liner notes and rarely-seen photos provide context. Available on Amazon or the Smithsonian Folkways site.
