Title: luke hatfield, developer

----

Bio: Luke has always enjoyed the intersection of design and technology. He dabbled in architecture as well as computer programming in college. But it wasn’t until after he’d served seven years in the US Army that he found his passion for web design and coding. He's pursuing a Master’s from Harvard Extension School in Digital Media and Instructional Design where he’s honed skills as a UX designer, developer, and video editor. He joined the Visual Dialogue team in 2014.
