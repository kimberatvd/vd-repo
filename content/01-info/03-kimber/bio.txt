Title: kimber lynch, senior designer/developer

----

Bio: Kimber is a designer/developer with a background and interest in both print and interactive design. A graduate from Massachusetts College of Art and Design with departmental honors, she was selected to show her work at the Art Director’s Club National Student Portfolio Review. She worked on the Grammy winning design for _Woody at 100: The Centennial Collection_ and  her work has been featured in _Communication Arts_, Creative Review, Packaging of the World, and Art of the Menu.
